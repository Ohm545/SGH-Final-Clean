1. Introduction

Briefly introduce the project.

Explain the purpose of the project and its relevance to the Dang District.

Mention the need for a smart complaint tracking system and how it aligns with the Smart Gujarat Hackathon's goals.

"Grievance Express is a smart complaint tracking and disposal system designed to address the increasing burden of day-to-day complaints in Dang District. The system aims to streamline the process of complaint resolution, ensuring accountability, transparency, and timely disposal of grievances. By leveraging technology, Grievance Express empowers citizens to report issues effortlessly while enabling officials to track and resolve complaints efficiently."

  • Time-consuming and inefficient processes.
  • Difficulty for citizens to report and track complaints.

  • To reduce the burden on officials through automation.

6. Features

List the key features of your system:

  • Multi-language support (Gujarati and English).
  • Voice-to-text and audio descriptions for accessibility.
  • Real-time complaint tracking for citizens.
  • Statistics dashboard for public accountability.
  • Role-based access for officials (Mamlatdar, SDM, Collector).
  • Future AI-based ranking system for departments.

  • Backend: Database for storing complaints, departments, and status updates.
