Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a complaint?

Click on the Citizen Portal button and follow the simple 3-step process

How long does resolution take?

It depends on the goverment Officials working on the case

What types of complaints can I file?

You can file any type of comaplaint, It will be automatically sent to the autorized person

Can I track the status of my complaint?

Yes, you will receive status notifications and expected completion dates via SMS or email.

What if I don't receive an OTP during verification?

Make sure your mobile number is correct and has network coverage. You can also request to resend the OTP.

How does Complaint Express ensure transparency?

We provide public access to case statistics, enhancing transparency and accountability in governance.

Can I view complaint statistics of my area?

Yes, you can view statistics and reviews based on districts, towns, or talukas.

Who can see my complaint?

Only relevant department heads can view your complaint. The district collector has access to all SDM complaints.

Will I be notified when my complaint is resolved?

Yes, you will receive a notification when your complaint is addressed.

Can I file complaints on behalf of someone else?

No, complaints must be filed by the person facing the issue to maintain accuracy and responsibility.

What should I do if my complaint is not resolved on time?

You can follow up directly through the platform or contact the relevant department using the provided contact details.

Can I edit or withdraw my complaint after submission?

No, complaints cannot be edited or withdrawn once submitted. Please ensure all information is accurate before submission.
